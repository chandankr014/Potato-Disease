Potato Disease Prediction

Project Description

Welcome to the Potato Disease Prediction project! This project aims to utilize data science and machine learning techniques to predict diseases in potato plants. Potatoes are vulnerable to various diseases that can significantly impact crop yield and quality. Early detection of these diseases is crucial for effective disease management and prevention.

Our project uses advanced image processing algorithms and machine learning models to analyze images of potato leaves and identify signs of disease. By training the model on a diverse dataset of healthy and diseased potato plants, we can create a reliable tool for farmers and researchers to predict and address potential disease outbreaks.

Potato Plant Conditions

  • Early Blight
  • Healthy
  • Late Blight

Feel free to explore our web application, where you can upload images of potato leaves to get predictions about potential diseases. Our goal is to empower farmers with accurate and timely information, helping them make informed decisions and safeguard their potato crops.
